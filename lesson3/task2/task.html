"chars".join(string or list) allows you to join together letters or items in a list, putting "chars" in between each one

For example:
word = "hello"
"*".join(word) = "h*e*l*l*o"

list = ["lion", "tigers", "bears"]
" and ".join(list) = "lion and tigers and bears"
